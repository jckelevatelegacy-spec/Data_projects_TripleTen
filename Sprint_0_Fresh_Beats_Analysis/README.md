Fresh Beats User Engagement & Promotion Analysis (Google Sheets / BI Project)

The goal of this project was to evaluate user engagement, artist promotion effectiveness, and content strategy for Fresh Beats, a music streaming platform focused on promoting emerging artists. The analysis was presented as a stakeholder-ready report, combining curated insights, visualizations, and business recommendations.

The Data

The project was based on a structured dataset and a set of pre-built visualizations provided within Google Sheets. Each dataset and chart represented key aspects of platform performance.

Core Data Areas Included:

User activity and engagement metrics (e.g., listens, sessions, interactions)
Subscription behavior (free vs. paid users)
Artist promotion performance metrics
Genre-level performance and listener preferences

Visualizations Provided:

Engagement trends over time
Conversion rates from free to paid users
Performance comparisons across genres and artists
Campaign effectiveness for promoted artists

Rather than building the analysis from scratch, the focus was on interpreting existing data and selecting the most relevant insights to address business challenges.

The Process
1. Understanding Business Objectives

I began by reviewing the company’s key challenges:

Improving artist promotion effectiveness
Increasing user engagement and subscription conversion
Enhancing genre-based recommendations for emerging artists

This step ensured that all selected insights directly aligned with stakeholder priorities.

2. Insight Selection & Interpretation

I analyzed the provided visualizations and datasets to identify the most meaningful patterns and trends. The focus was on:

Identifying which promotional strategies led to higher artist visibility
Understanding user behavior patterns that drive engagement and retention
Evaluating which genres perform best among different user segments

Rather than performing calculations, the emphasis was on connecting data insights to business impact.

3. Report Development

I structured the analysis into a concise, stakeholder-friendly report using a predefined template. This included:

A status summary highlighting key findings and their business implications
Organized sections aligned with each business challenge
Embedded visualizations to support each insight
Clear, actionable recommendations based on the analysis

Special attention was given to clarity, structure, and readability to ensure the report could be easily understood by non-technical stakeholders.

Results

The analysis identified several key drivers of platform performance:

Certain promotional strategies were more effective in increasing artist visibility and engagement, indicating opportunities to optimize marketing efforts
User engagement patterns revealed clear behaviors associated with higher likelihood of conversion to paid subscriptions
Genre-level insights showed that aligning recommendations with user preferences can significantly improve engagement and discovery of new artists

By aligning insights with business challenges, the report provided a clear framework for improving both user experience and revenue generation.

Conclusion

This project strengthened my ability to work as a Business Intelligence Analyst by focusing on insight selection, data interpretation, and stakeholder communication. I developed experience in translating data into clear business narratives and actionable recommendations without relying on complex calculations.

The final deliverable demonstrates my ability to connect data insights to strategic business decisions and present findings in a professional, stakeholder-ready format.
